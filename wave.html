WAVE Record Label

WAVE is a record label based in China. It provides instant access to Chinese underground Hip-Hop music and international exposure for Chinese underground Hip-Hop artists. As a record label, it reaches out to underground Hip-Hop artists in China who are at the beginning of their careers and need a platform to promote their artworks.
